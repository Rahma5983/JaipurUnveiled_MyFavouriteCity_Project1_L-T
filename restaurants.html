Chokhi Dhani - (Click Here)
Ethic Village Resort

Experience authentic Rajasthani culture, folk dances, puppet shows, and traditional food served in a village setting.

  • Cuisine: Rajasthani Thali
  • Speciality: Complete cultural experience, traditional dining.
  • Ratings: 4.5/5
  • Reviews: "A must-do for anyone visiting Jaipur! The food and entertainment are fantastic."

Spice Court - (Click Here)
Fine Dining, Local Delicacies

Known for its traditional Rajasthani and North Indian cuisine, particularly the famous Laal Maas, in a comfortable setting with live music.

  • Cuisine: Rajasthani, North Indian
  • Speciality: Laal Maas, Ker Sangri.
  • Ratings: 4.4/5
  • Reviews: "Best Laal Maas in Jaipur! Excellent service and ambiance."

Lassiwala (MI Road) - (Click Here)
Iconic Lassi Shop

A historic shop famous for its refreshing, thick lassi served in traditional earthen pots (kulhads). A perfect treat in Jaipur's heat.

  • Cuisine: Beverages
  • Speciality: Sweet Lassi, Malai Lassi.
  • Ratings: 4.6/5
  • Reviews: "The best lassi I've ever had! Simple, authentic, and incredibly delicious."
