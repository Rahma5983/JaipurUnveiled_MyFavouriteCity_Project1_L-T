The Art of Quartz

Blue Pottery is a unique art form from Jaipur, made using quartz instead of clay. Look for vases, tiles, and decorative items.

  • Speciality: Quartz-based ceramic, vibrant blue-green hues.
  • Best Place: Kripal Kumbh or shops in Sanganer.
  • Ratings: 4.7/5 (Experience)

View Forts from Above

Get a spectacular, bird's-eye view of the Amber Fort and the Pink City's landscapes at sunrise. A truly unforgettable adventure!

  • Speciality: Sunrise views over Amer, Nahargarh Forts.
  • Time: Early Morning (Seasonal)
  • Reviews: "A magical experience! Worth the early wake-up."

Jewellery & Gemstone Shopping - (Click Here)
Jewellery Hub of India

Jaipur is famous for cutting and polishing precious stones. Johari Bazaar is the epicenter for finding exquisite Kundan and Meenakari jewellery.

  • Speciality: Kundan, Meenakari, and Polki jewellery.
  • Best Place: Johari Bazaar and Tripolia Bazaar.
  • Ratings: 4.5/5 (Bazaar Experience)
